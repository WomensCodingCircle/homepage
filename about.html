About the Women's Coding Circle

In 2014, Charlotte Weaver and Kristen Nyce, software engineers at Janelia Research Campus, took note of the lack of women represented among programmers. They decided to take action by providing an atmosphere where other women in the Janelia community would be able to connect, learn, and share knowledge about coding languages.

The group of over 40 women meets several times per month to practice and teach each other about various languages, such as Python and html. Learn more about our projects here.
